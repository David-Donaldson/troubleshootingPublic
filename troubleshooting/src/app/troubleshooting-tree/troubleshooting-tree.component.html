5GHz vs. 2.5 GHz Wireless Settings

Flow fibre modems/ gateway devices supports two (2) wireless frequencies or networks, 2.5GHz and 5GHz dual band wireless settings.

  • 5GHz offers more throughput [faster speeds] with less range, ideal for gaming, streaming
  • 2.5 GHz offers more range with less throughput ideal for browsing, streaming SD content etc.
